Welcome user, this is the web page we created.


Choose an operation:
  1. Record the numerical metadata consisting of the number of rows and columns of each of the two files composing the data collection.



  2. Record the general semantics of the dataset, i.e. the labels of the columns of each of the two files composing the data collection.



  3. Record the number of different genes detected in literature. The list is sorted in ascending order.



  4. Given a gene symbol, provide the list of sentences that are an evidence in literature about the relation of this gene with COVID-19.



  5. Record the number of different diseases detected in literature. The list is sorted in ascending order.



  6. Given a disease ID, provide the list of sentences that are an evidence in literature about the relation of this disease with COVID-19.



  7. Record the 10-top most frequent distinct association between genes and diseases.



  8. Given a gene symbol, provide the list of diseases such a gene is associated with.



  9. Given a disease ID, provide the list of genes such a disease is associated with.
